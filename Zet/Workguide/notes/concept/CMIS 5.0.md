up:: [[Common Management Interface Specification (CMIS)]]

- [[CMIS5p0.pdf]]

- CMIS 5.0 was developed by QSFP-DD, OSFP and COBO
- Gray Coding: used with PAM4 modulation.
	- (0,0) maps to level 0
	- (0,1) maps to level 1
	- (1,1) maps to level 2
	- (1,0) maps to level 3
