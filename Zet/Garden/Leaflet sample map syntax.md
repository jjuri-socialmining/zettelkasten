Simple style
```leaflet 
id: leaflet-map 
image: [[20221008114500.png]] 
```

Advanced style
```leaflet 
id: leaflet-map 
image: [[20221008114500.png]] 
height: 500px 
lat: 50 
long: 50 
minZoom: 1 
maxZoom: 10 
defaultZoom: 5 
unit: meters 
scale: 1 
marker: default, 39.983334, -82.983330, [[Note]] 
darkMode: false
```
